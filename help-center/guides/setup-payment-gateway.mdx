title: Setting Up Vandar Payment Gateway
description: A simple guide to enable Vandar payments.
---

## Overview

Integrate Vandar to accept payments in your app.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Visit dashboard, generate your key in the API section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send payment request">
    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://api.vandar.io/v1/payments \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -d '{"amount":10000}'
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle redirect">
    Users are redirected to payment page, then back with result.
  </Step>
</Steps>
