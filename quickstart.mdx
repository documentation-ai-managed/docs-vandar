## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for version control integration
</Callout>

## Step 1: Create Your Vandar Account

Sign up for Vandar in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Vandar Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to the [Vandar dashboard](https://app.vandar.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, password, and workspace name. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Select your plan (free tier available) and customize your brand color (`#3B82F6` for Vandar blue).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Create a project space to organize your docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="folder-plus">
    Click **New Space** in the dashboard sidebar.
    
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select a template (e.g., API Reference or Product Docs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    Set your space slug (e.g., `my-project-docs`), enable search, and add custom domains if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Upload Your Initial Documentation Files

Add your first MDX files using multiple methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="upload">
    Open your space editor and drag MDX, Markdown, or image files directly into the file tree.
    
    <Image
      src="https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/3B82F6/white?text=Drag+%26+Drop+Files"
      alt="Drag and drop files into Vandar editor"
      width="800"
      height="400"
    />
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub Sync" icon="github">
    Connect your GitHub repo:
    
````bash
# In your space settings, paste your repo URL
https://github.com/your-username/vandar-docs
````
    
    Vandar auto-syncs changes on push.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manual Upload" icon="cloud-upload">
    Use the **Upload** button to select files from your local drive.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 5: Publish Your First Doc Page

Make your docs live.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write Sample Content" icon="edit">
    Create `quickstart.mdx` with frontmatter:
    
````mdx
```mdx
---
title: "My First Page"
description: "Sample Vandar docs page"
---
```
````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Edit" icon="eye">
    Use the split view to validate components and formatting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="rocket">
    Click **Publish** > **All Changes**. Your docs are now live at `https://docs.vandar.com/my-project-docs/quickstart`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success" title="Congratulations!">
You've successfully set up Vandar and published your first documentation space. Customize further with themes, integrations, and advanced MDX components.
</Callout>
